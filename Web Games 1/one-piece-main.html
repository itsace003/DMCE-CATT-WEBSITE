In this anime called ONE PIECE, 22 years ago, the Pirate King, Gol D. Roger, was executed. However, before his death he revealed to everyone that his treasure. The ONE PIECE, was hidden at the end of the Grand Line. This inspired people to be pirates and sail towards the treasure, beginning th Great Age of Pirates.

The story follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy, a boy whose body gained properties of rubber after unintentionally eating “ Gomu gomu” a devil fruit and his brother Portgas D. Ace, whose body gained properties of fire after intentionally eating “Mera mera no Mi” devil fruit. They both have their crews named as “Straw hat pirates” of Luffy and “Spade pirates” of Ace.
